Manipur Tour Packages

Package 1: Loktak Lake Expedition

Embark on a mesmerizing journey to Loktak Lake, the largest freshwater lake in Northeast India, spanning over 40 square kilometers. Over 4 days, experience the beauty of floating islands, pristine waters, and unique phumdis (floating biomass) while immersing yourself in the rich biodiversity of the region.

Duration: 4 Days

Price per Person: ₹6499

Your package includes 4 days and 3 nights of accommodation in comfortable lakeside resorts or eco-friendly homestays. Indulge in delicious meals prepared with fresh local ingredients, showcasing Manipur's diverse culinary heritage. Transportation for all sightseeing activities and transfers is provided, ensuring a hassle-free experience. Expert local guides will accompany you on excursions, offering insights into the lake's ecosystem and the culture of nearby communities. Entrance fees to attractions mentioned in the itinerary are covered, allowing you to fully explore Loktak Lake and its surroundings.

Day 1: Arrival in Imphal, Transfer to Loktak Lake
Day 2: Explore Loktak Lake and Keibul Lamjao National Park
Day 3: Visit Sendra Island and Loktak Floating Resort
Day 4: Departure from Loktak Lake

Package 2: Shirui Lily Trek

Embark on an adventurous trek to witness the enchanting Shirui Lily, a rare and endemic flower found only in the hills of Manipur. Over 5 days, trek through lush forests, cascading waterfalls, and serene meadows, culminating in the breathtaking sight of thousands of blooming lilies painting the landscape with vibrant colors.

Duration: 5 Days

Price per Person: ₹7999

Your package includes 5 days and 4 nights of camping under the stars, providing an immersive experience amidst nature. All camping equipment, including tents, sleeping bags, and cooking facilities, are provided. Expert trekking guides will lead you through the trails, ensuring safety and sharing insights into the local flora and fauna. Permits for trekking and village visits are included.

Day 1: Arrival in Ukhrul, Camp setup and orientation
Day 2: Trek to Shirui Peak and Nillai Tea Estate
Day 3: Explore Khangkhui Mangsor Cave and Kachouphung Lake
Day 4: Visit Khayang Waterfalls and Tangkhul Naga villages
Day 5: Departure from Ukhrul

Package 4: Keibul Lamjao Wildlife Safari

Embark on an exhilarating wildlife safari adventure in Keibul Lamjao National Park, the last natural refuge of the endangered Sangai deer. Over 4 days, explore the diverse ecosystems of the park, including marshy grasslands, floating phumdis, and serene lakes, while spotting a variety of rare flora and fauna.

Duration: 4 Days

Price per Person: ₹6999

Your package includes 4 days and 3 nights of accommodation in cozy lodges or eco-resorts near Keibul Lamjao National Park. Enjoy guided safari tours led by experienced naturalists, offering insights into the park's biodiversity and conservation efforts. Transportation for safari activities and park entrances fees are included, ensuring a seamless wildlife viewing experience.

Day 1: Arrival in Imphal, Transfer to Keibul Lamjao National Park
Day 2: Morning and Evening Wildlife Safari in the Park
Day 3: Explore Loktak Lake and Sangai Conservation Center
Day 4: Departure from Keibul Lamjao National Park
