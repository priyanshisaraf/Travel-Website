Assam Tour Packages

Package 1: Kaziranga Wildlife Safari

Embark on an exciting 3-day safari adventure in Kaziranga National Park, home to the iconic one-horned rhinoceros, where you'll encounter diverse wildlife amidst the lush greenery of Assam's famed wilderness.

Duration: 3 Days

Price per Person: ₹5499

Experience the thrill of wildlife encounters with 3 days and 2 nights of accommodation in cozy resorts near Kaziranga National Park. Enjoy delicious meals featuring Assamese cuisine and beverages. Transportation for safari activities and transfers is provided. Knowledgeable naturalists will accompany you on safari drives, offering insights into the park's biodiversity. Park entry fees and safari charges are included for a memorable wildlife experience.

Day 1: Arrival in Kaziranga, Afternoon Safari
Day 2: Morning and Afternoon Safari in Kaziranga
Day 3: Departure from Kaziranga

Package 2: Majuli Island Retreat

Escape to the serene beauty of Majuli, the world's largest river island, during this 4-day retreat where you'll discover ancient monasteries, interact with local artisans, and witness breathtaking sunsets over the Brahmaputra River.

Duration: 4 Days

Price per Person: ₹4999

Experience tranquility with 4 days and 3 nights of accommodation in traditional cottages or guesthouses on Majuli Island. Indulge in authentic Assamese cuisine with locally sourced ingredients. Transportation for island exploration and transfers is provided. Engage with local artisans and cultural performers, gaining insights into Majuli's vibrant heritage. Boat rides on the Brahmaputra River and visits to ancient monasteries are included for a memorable retreat experience.

Day 1: Arrival in Majuli, Visit to Satras
Day 2: Explore Majuli Island, Interact with artisans
Day 3: Sunset at Brahmaputra River, Cultural Performances
Day 4: Departure from Majuli
